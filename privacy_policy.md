# Privacy Policy for Imposter

**Effective Date:** February 21, 2026  
**Developer:** Surajit Das  
**App Name:** Imposter – Social Deduction Game  
**Package ID:** com.game.impstr

---

## Overview

Imposter is a local, offline social deduction game designed to be played on a single shared Android device. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

**The short version: Imposter does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.**

---

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. Specifically:

- **No personal information** — The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
- **No usage data** — The app does not track how you use it, how often you play, or what in-app choices you make.
- **No location data** — The app does not access your device's location.
- **No device information** — The app does not read or transmit any device identifiers, hardware details, or system information.
- **No camera or microphone access** — The app does not request or use these permissions.
- **No contacts or files** — The app does not access your contacts, media, or file storage.

---

## Internet and Network Access

Imposter is a **fully offline application**. It does not connect to the internet under any circumstances. There are no servers, no cloud sync, no analytics endpoints, and no third-party network calls.

---

## Data Storage

The app uses **Android SharedPreferences** solely to remember your last-used player configuration (e.g., player names entered before a game session) for convenience within the app. This data:

- Is stored **locally on your device only**
- Is **never transmitted** anywhere
- Can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data in your Android device settings (Settings → Apps → Imposter → Clear Data)

No databases are stored in the cloud or on any external server.

---

## Third-Party Services

Imposter does **not** integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

- Analytics platforms (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics)
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Social media SDKs
- Payment processors

---

## Permissions

Imposter does **not request any Android permissions**. It operates entirely without access to any sensitive device features or system resources.

---

## Children's Privacy

Imposter is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no data of any kind, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws worldwide. We do not knowingly (or unknowingly) collect information from children or adults.

---

## Advertising

Imposter contains **no advertisements**. No ad SDKs are included, and no advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) are accessed or used.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the app is updated in a way that changes data practices, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and the new effective date will be reflected at the top of this document. Since the app currently collects no data, any such change would only occur if new features are introduced in a future version.
